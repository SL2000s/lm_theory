Lemma 1: Lipschitz Composition Lemma

Statement

Let $g,h$ be two composable Lipschitz functions. Then $g \circ h$ is also Lipschitz with $\lip(g \circ h) \leq \lip(g) \lip(h)$.

Motivation of Statement (AI-Generated)

The composition of Lipschitz functions theorem is useful in analysis and applied mathematics, particularly when dealing with functions that have bounded rates of change. If you know that two functions $g$ and $h$ are Lipschitz, this theorem allows you to conclude that their composition $g \circ h$ is also Lipschitz. This is particularly valuable in scenarios where you need to ensure that the composed function does not exhibit wild fluctuations, which is crucial in stability analysis, numerical methods, and various applications in differential equations and optimization.
